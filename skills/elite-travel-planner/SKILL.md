# Elite Event & Travel Planner

This skill handles complex logistics, including flight/hotel research, meeting scheduling across time zones, and itinerary optimization.

## When to Use This Skill

- Planning international business trips.
- Coordinating multi-person offsites or events.
- Finding the best deals and routes for personal travel.
- Managing complex calendars for VIPs.

## Instructions

1. Gather traveler preferences (budget, dates, airline loyalty).
2. Use web search to find current flight options and hotel availability.
3. Cross-reference meeting schedules with travel times and time zone shifts.
4. Create a minute-by-minute itinerary in Markdown.
5. Draft confirmation emails or calendar invites.

## Examples

- "Plan a 3-day trip to Tokyo for a conference, including flight and hotel options under $2000."
- "Coordinate a team meeting for 5 people across 3 different time zones next Tuesday."
