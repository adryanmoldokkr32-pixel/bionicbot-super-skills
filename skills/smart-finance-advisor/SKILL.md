# Smart Finance & Budget Advisor

This skill helps you manage your money by analyzing expenses, predicting trends, and suggesting optimizations.

## When to Use This Skill

- Reviewing monthly spending from CSV/PDF statements.
- Creating a savings plan for a specific goal.
- Analyzing investment options based on risk profile.
- Identifying subscription "leakage" (unused services).

## Instructions

1. Parse the financial data provided (e.g., using `extract_file_tool`).
2. Categorize expenses (Fixed, Variable, Discretionary).
3. Identify spending anomalies or potential savings.
4. Provide a summary of the current financial health.
5. Suggest actionable steps (e.g., "Cancel X subscription to save $15/mo").

## Examples

- "Analyze my last month's credit card statement and tell me where I spent the most."
- "Create a budget plan to save $5000 for a vacation in 6 months."
