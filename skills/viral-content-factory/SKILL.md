# Viral Content Factory

This skill transforms raw ideas or links into high-performance social media posts optimized for engagement and reach.

## When to Use This Skill

- Creating a thread from a complex article.
- Drafting LinkedIn posts for professional reach.
- Optimizing "hooks" for maximum click-through rates.
- Generating daily content ideas based on trending topics.

## Instructions

1. Analyze the input content (article, raw notes, or topic).
2. Identify the core "aha!" moment or controversial take.
3. Generate 3 variants of "hooks" (first sentences).
4. Draft a structured post/thread following platform-specific best practices (e.g., X threads, LinkedIn long-form).
5. Include relevant, non-spammy hashtags and call-to-actions.

## Examples

- "Turn this blog post about AI agents into a 5-tweet thread for X."
- "Write a LinkedIn post about my recent promotion at Emergent."
