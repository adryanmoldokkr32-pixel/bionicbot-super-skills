# Deep Research & Synthesis

This skill enables bionicbot to perform in-depth, multi-step research on complex topics and provide a high-level executive summary.

## When to Use This Skill

- Investigating new market trends.
- In-depth technical comparisons.
- Gathering primary-source evidence for reports.
- Synthesizing state-of-the-art research in a specific field.

## Instructions

1. Identify the core research questions from the user's prompt.
2. Use the `deep_research_assistant` tool to gather comprehensive information.
3. If the results are broad, perform follow-up searches on specific sub-topics.
4. Synthesize the findings into an executive summary.
5. Include a "Key Takeaways" section and a "Sources" section with full URLs.

## Examples

- "Research the current state of Solid State Batteries in 2026 and summarize the top 3 players."
- "Perform a deep dive into the regulatory changes for AI in the EU for the next 2 years."
