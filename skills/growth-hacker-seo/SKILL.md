# SEO & Growth Hacker

This skill optimizes digital content for search engines and suggests viral growth strategies to increase traffic and conversion.

## When to Use This Skill

- Auditing a landing page for SEO performance.
- Keyword research for a new blog post.
- Analyzing competitor growth tactics.
- Improving website meta-tags and descriptions.

## Instructions

1. Crawl the target URL using `crawl_tool`.
2. Analyze meta-titles, descriptions, H1 headers, and keyword density.
3. Suggest 3 high-impact SEO improvements.
4. Propose a "growth hack" (e.g., referral program, viral loop).
5. Provide a list of target keywords with estimated difficulty.

## Examples

- "Audit my portfolio website for SEO and suggest 3 ways to rank higher."
- "Give me 10 low-competition keywords for the 'AI budget tools' niche."
